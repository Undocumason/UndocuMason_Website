Purpose

Our purpose statement


Our student run organization, UndocuMason formerly known as Mason DREAMers. This name change brings us to a new chapter and we want to take a moment of gratitude for the members who came before us that have helped push us to where we are today. We hope that with this name change we can continue to move forward in the fight to liberate all our undocumented brothers and sisters. We want to thank our brave alumni who founded Mason DREAMers in 2011, without their drive for our mission statement, we would not be here today. Our purpose is to advocate and create an inclusive environment for all 11+ million undocumented immigrants in the United States. The term DREAMers fails to encompass all immigrants living in this country. This narrative strengthens the “model minority” immigrant stereotype which has been used to refer to a minority group perceived as particularly successful, especially in a manner that contrasts with other minority groups. Many use this to rationalize discrimination against some immigrants while it can tokenize others. The purpose of UndocuMason is to spread our mission statement, which is to “create a more inclusive environment for undocumented immigrants through education and advocacy.”

Undocumason's Goals

UndocuMason is a student-run organization with the purpose of creating an inclusive environment for undocumented immigrants through education and advocacy. Our goal this school year is to spread our mission statement, internally and externally. External meaning conducting outreaches with local high schools and community colleges, maintaining DMV/East coast alliance. Internally in a way where we build a community between undocumented students with support from other student organizations on campus.

Undocumented Traveling

Tips


- Remember that your feelings are valid and that the thought of traveling may look differently for each individual. There are a plethora of feelings that each person goes through so your feelings are completely valid.

- Talk to people you trust to ensure you have a strong support system to benefit you in a way where they could help you decrease stress or even look into information about the airline, airport, train station, etc for you, to help alleviate stress. They’re your ally and want you to travel in a safe way.

- Always be prepared for anything. Prepare for the best and worst case scenario for whatever happens and make sure that you have reliable people to help you through it. If something goes wrong, you’re prepared and have people ready to help you, if something goes right, you have a support system to exclaim the great news to.

- Familiarize yourself with all of the traveling procedures. Familiarizing yourself with where and how you will be traveling will help you prepare in advance. For a more thorough explanation to get started, please refer to our alliance organization @immigrantsrising at “immigrantsrising.org/travel-us”
